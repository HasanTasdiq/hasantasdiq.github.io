I am S. Mahmudul Hasan, a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Tulane University, working with Prof. Lu Peng on AI, systems, and edge devices. I hold an M.S. from Syracuse University (2024), with research in security, privacy, and usable security, and a B.Sc. from BUET (2018). Prior to my Ph.D., I taught as a Lecturer at the Canadian University of Bangladesh and Eastern University.
